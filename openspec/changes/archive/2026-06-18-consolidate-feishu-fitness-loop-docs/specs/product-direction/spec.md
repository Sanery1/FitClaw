## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: First-stage Feishu loop status documentation
FitClaw SHALL keep a concise status document for the first-stage Feishu fitness loop.

#### Scenario: Reviewing first-stage Feishu readiness
- **WHEN** contributors need to understand current Feishu fitness-loop coverage and remaining gaps
- **THEN** they SHALL be able to find a status document that distinguishes deterministic eval coverage from live Feishu validation and points to recommended next steps
